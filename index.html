Our Services

Comprehensive specialist services for the diagnosis, assessment and ongoing management of neurological and developmental conditions in children.

Paediatric Neurology

Our paediatric neurology service provides comprehensive assessment, diagnosis and ongoing management for a wide range of neurological conditions in children. Our experienced paediatric neurologists use the latest evidence-based approaches to care for children with complex neurological needs.

We work closely with families to develop individualised management plans, coordinating with allied health professionals and schools to ensure the best outcomes for every child. Our neurologists have access to on-site EEG and EMG services, enabling efficient and comprehensive diagnostic workups.

Developmental Paediatrics

Our developmental paediatrics team provides thorough assessments for children who may be experiencing delays or differences in their development. We take a holistic, family-centred approach to understanding each child's unique developmental profile.

Our multidisciplinary team includes developmental paediatricians and psychologists who work together to provide comprehensive assessments. Following assessment, we develop tailored management plans and connect families with appropriate early intervention services and support.

Paediatric EEG

Paedswest offers private paediatric EEG (electroencephalogram) services in a child-friendly environment. An EEG is a non-invasive test that records electrical activity in the brain and is an essential diagnostic tool for evaluating seizures, epilepsy and other neurological conditions in children.

Our dedicated clinical neurophysiology scientist ensures that every EEG is conducted in a supportive, child-friendly environment. We understand that medical tests can be daunting for children and their families, and we take every step to make the experience as comfortable as possible. Results are reviewed by our paediatric neurologists and discussed with families in a timely manner.

Paediatric EMG / Nerve Conduction Studies

Our EMG (electromyography) and nerve conduction study service provides specialised electrophysiological testing for children with suspected neuromuscular or peripheral nerve disorders. These tests help to diagnose conditions affecting the nerves and muscles.

EMG and nerve conduction studies are performed by our experienced paediatric neurologists with expertise in electrophysiology. We take a gentle, patient approach to ensure children are as comfortable as possible during the procedure. Results are interpreted in the context of each child's clinical presentation and discussed with families promptly.

Neonatal & Perinatal Medicine

Our neonatal and perinatal medicine service provides specialist consultation and developmental follow-up for infants who have experienced complications during the neonatal period, including premature babies and those with birth-related challenges.

Our neonatologist and developmental paediatricians work together to provide comprehensive follow-up for infants at risk of developmental difficulties. Early identification and intervention are key to optimising outcomes, and we work closely with families to monitor developmental milestones and connect them with appropriate support services.

Telehealth Services

Paedswest is committed to ensuring that families across Western Australia have access to specialist paediatric neurology and developmental services, regardless of their location. Our telehealth service provides remote consultations via secure video link.

Telehealth consultations are available for both initial assessments (where clinically appropriate) and follow-up appointments. Our telehealth service is particularly valuable for families in regional and remote areas of Western Australia who may face significant travel to access specialist care in Perth.

ABOUT US

About Paedswest

A leading paediatric neurology and developmental clinic providing specialist care for children and families across Western Australia.

Dedicated to Children's Neurological Health

Paedswest is a specialist paediatric neurology and developmental clinic located in Mount Lawley, Perth. Our multidisciplinary team provides expert assessment, diagnosis and ongoing management for a wide range of neurological and developmental conditions in children.

Founded with a commitment to delivering the highest standard of paediatric care, Paedswest brings together experienced paediatric neurologists, developmental paediatricians, a clinical neurophysiology scientist, and allied health professionals. We believe that every child deserves access to expert, compassionate care.

Our clinic is equipped with dedicated paediatric EEG and EMG services, enabling comprehensive diagnostic workups in a child-friendly environment. We also offer telehealth consultations for families across regional Western Australia, ensuring that distance is never a barrier to accessing specialist care.

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by our commitment to providing exceptional, compassionate care for every child and family.

Compassionate Care

We understand the concerns families face when seeking specialist care for their child. Every interaction is guided by empathy and understanding.

Family-Centred Approach

We partner with families at every step, ensuring parents are informed, supported and empowered in their child's care journey.

Clinical Excellence

Our team upholds the highest standards of medical practice, drawing on extensive training and ongoing professional development.

Evidence-Based Practice

All treatment and management plans are grounded in the latest clinical research and best-practice guidelines.
